Classic Waffle

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of flour (wheat)
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 spoons corn starch
  • 3 beaten eggs
  • 4 tablespoons melted unsalted butter
  • 1 and 3/4 cup (tea) milk
  • tablespoon of vanilla essence

Method of preparation

  1. Sift the flour, sugar, starch, yeast and salt into a bowl. Reserve.
  2. In another container, mix the beaten eggs with the milk, the melted butter and the vanilla essence.
  3. Pour over the flour mixture and quickly incorporate the ingredients.
  4. Heat up the waffle iron. Place a shallow ladle of dough and spread until it covers the appliance mold, close the lid and bake until the dough is golden brown.
  5. Remove with silicone spatulas. Serve with honey, fruit or jam.
